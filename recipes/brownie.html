Brownie Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • Choco chips or nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  • Preheat oven to 175┬░C (350┬░F). Grease a square baking pan.
  • In a bowl, mix melted butter and sugar until smooth.
  • Add eggs and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  • Stir in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  • Pour batter into the pan, spread evenly, and sprinkle choco chips or nuts if using.
  • Bake for 20тАУ25 minutes. Let it cool before cutting into squares. Enjoy!
