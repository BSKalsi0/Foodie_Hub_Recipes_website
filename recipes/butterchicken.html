Butter Chicken Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp red chili powder, garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt, oil

Instructions:

  • Marinate chicken in yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste for 1 hour.
  • Cook marinated chicken until slightly golden.
  • In another pan, saut├й onions in butter. Add tomato puree and cook well.
  • Add cream, kasuri methi, and spices. Simmer for 5 mins.
  • Add cooked chicken and simmer for 10 more mins.
  • Serve hot with naan or rice.
