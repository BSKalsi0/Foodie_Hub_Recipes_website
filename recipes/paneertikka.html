Paneer Tikka Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  • Mix yogurt, spices, ginger-garlic paste, and lemon juice to make marinade.
  • Add paneer cubes and coat well. Marinate for at least 1 hour.
  • Grill, bake, or pan-fry the paneer until golden.
  • Serve with mint chutney and onions.
