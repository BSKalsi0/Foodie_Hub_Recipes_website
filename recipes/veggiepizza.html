Veggie Pizza

Ingredients (English)

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers (green, red, yellow)
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn
  • Oregano and chili flakes to taste

Instructions (English)

  • Preheat oven to 220┬░C (428┬░F).
  • Spread pizza sauce on the base.
  • Add a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  • Top with all vegetables evenly.
  • Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes or until cheese melts and crust is golden.
