Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 cups tomato pasta sauce
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, oregano, basil to taste

Instructions:

  • Boil lasagna sheets as per instructions and set aside.
  • In a pan, heat olive oil, saut├й onion and garlic, add pasta sauce, salt, pepper, herbs and simmer.
  • In a baking dish, layer sauce, lasagna sheets, ricotta, mozzarella, and repeat layers.
  • Top with parmesan cheese and bake at 180┬░C (350┬░F) for 30-35 minutes.
  • Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving hot.
