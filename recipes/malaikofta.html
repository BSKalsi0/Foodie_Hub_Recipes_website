Malai Kofta Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer, grated
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp cream

Instructions:

  • Mix paneer, potato, corn flour, salt and make balls. Fry until golden.
  • Saut├й onion, ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree in oil.
  • Add spices and cream. Simmer sauce and add koftas before serving.
