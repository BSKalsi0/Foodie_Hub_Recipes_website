Samosa Recipe

üá¨üáß English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Water as needed
  • 2 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  • Mix flour, oil and salt; knead into a dough using water. Rest for 20 mins.
  • Heat oil, add cumin, peas, potatoes, masalas. Mix and cool stuffing.
  • Roll dough, cut in half, form cone, fill with stuffing and seal.
  • Deep fry until golden brown. Serve with chutney.
