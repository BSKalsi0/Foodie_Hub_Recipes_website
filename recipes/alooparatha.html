Aloo Paratha Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 chopped green chilies
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt and coriander leaves
  • Oil or ghee for roasting

Instructions:

  • Knead soft dough using wheat flour and water. Let it rest.
  • Mash potatoes and mix with onion, chilies, ginger, and spices.
  • Take dough, fill with potato mix, and roll into paratha.
  • Roast on tawa with oil/ghee till golden and crisp.
