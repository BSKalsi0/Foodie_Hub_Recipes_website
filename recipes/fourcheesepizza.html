Four Cheese Pizza

Ingredients (English)

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup gorgonzola or blue cheese
  • Oregano and chili flakes (optional)

Instructions (English)

  • Preheat oven to 220┬░C (428┬░F).
  • Spread tomato sauce on the pizza base.
  • Evenly sprinkle all four cheeses over the base.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes until cheese is bubbly and crust is golden.
  • Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes as desired. Serve hot.
