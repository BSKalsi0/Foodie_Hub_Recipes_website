Fish Fry Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish fillets
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp rice flour
  • 2 tbsp semolina (rava)
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions:

  • Clean the fish fillets thoroughly.
  • Marinate with ginger garlic paste, lemon juice, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let it rest for 30 mins.
  • Mix rice flour and semolina. Coat the marinated fish with this mixture.
  • Heat oil and shallow fry until golden brown on both sides.
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges and onion rings.
