Penne Arrabbiata Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 400g canned tomatoes (or 3 fresh tomatoes, chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  • Boil penne pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • In a pan, heat olive oil. Saut├й garlic and chili flakes until fragrant.
  • Add tomatoes, salt, and cook for 10-15 minutes until thickened.
  • Add cooked pasta and toss to coat in sauce.
  • Garnish with basil and parmesan. Serve hot!
