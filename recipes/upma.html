Upma Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 2.5 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves and lemon juice for garnish

Instructions:

  • Dry roast semolina until aromatic. Keep aside.
  • Heat oil, add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, curry leaves.
  • Add onions and green chilies, saut├й till soft.
  • Add water and salt, bring to boil.
  • Slowly add semolina while stirring to avoid lumps.
  • Cover and cook for 2-3 mins. Garnish and serve hot.
