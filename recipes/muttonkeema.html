Mutton Keema Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced mutton
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt as per taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  • Heat oil, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • Saut├й onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook.
  • Add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  • Add minced mutton and all the spices. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook for 20тАУ25 minutes on medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with roti or naan.
