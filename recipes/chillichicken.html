Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cubed
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp chili sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  • Marinate chicken with soy sauce, vinegar, flours, salt & pepper. Fry till golden.
  • Saut√© onion, chilies, and bell pepper in little oil.
  • Add sauces and fried chicken. Toss well to coat evenly.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes. Serve hot.
