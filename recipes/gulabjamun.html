Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Milk (as needed to make dough)
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • Rose water or saffron (optional)

Instructions:

  • Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee. Add milk gradually to form a soft dough. Rest for 10 minutes.
  • Meanwhile, boil sugar and water to make syrup. Add cardamom and rose water.
  • Make small smooth balls from dough.
  • Heat oil on low-medium flame. Fry balls till golden brown.
  • Soak fried balls in warm syrup for at least 1 hour before serving.
