Veg Kolhapuri Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, cauliflower, peas)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Kolhapuri masala or red chili masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  • Boil the mixed vegetables till slightly tender.
  • Heat oil in a pan, saut├й onions till golden, add ginger-garlic paste.
  • Add tomatoes, turmeric, Kolhapuri masala, and salt. Cook till oil separates.
  • Add the boiled vegetables and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • Finish with garam masala and serve hot with roti or naan.
