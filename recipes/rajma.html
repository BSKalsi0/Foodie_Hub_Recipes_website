Rajma Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup kidney beans (rajma), soaked overnight
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  • Pressure cook soaked rajma until soft (4-5 whistles).
  • Heat oil, add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and saut├й till golden.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and saut├й, then add tomato puree and spices.
  • Cook till oil separates, add cooked rajma and simmer for 15-20 mins.
  • Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice.
