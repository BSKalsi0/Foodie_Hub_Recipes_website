Mutton Korma Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton (bone-in)
  • 2 onions (fried and crushed)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tbsp oil/ghee
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)

Instructions:

  • Heat oil or ghee and add whole spices.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and saut├й until aromatic.
  • Add mutton and cook until lightly browned.
  • Add yogurt, turmeric, chili, and coriander powder.
  • Mix well and cook covered on low flame until mutton is tender.
  • Add crushed fried onions and simmer.
  • Sprinkle garam masala before serving.
  • Serve hot with naan or rice.
