Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sour curd
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 onion (optional), sliced
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup cooked rice

Instructions:

  • In a bowl, mix curd, besan, turmeric, salt, and 2 cups water. Whisk well.
  • Heat oil, add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and dry red chilies.
  • Optionally add onions and saut├й till golden.
  • Pour the curd mixture, stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  • Simmer on low flame for 15тАУ20 minutes till thickened.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice.
