Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients (English)

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20-25 pepperoni slices
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions (English)

  • Preheat oven to 220┬░C (428┬░F).
  • Spread pizza sauce over the base.
  • Top with mozzarella cheese evenly.
  • Add pepperoni slices over the cheese.
  • Sprinkle oregano and drizzle olive oil.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
