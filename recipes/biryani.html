Chicken Biryani Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cleaned
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, bay leaf, cinnamon)
  • 1 tsp turmeric, red chili, garam masala
  • Fresh coriander & mint
  • Salt, oil, and saffron milk

Instructions:

  • Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste. Rest 1 hour.
  • Fry onions till golden. Remove half for garnishing.
  • Add tomatoes and marinated chicken. Cook till chicken is done.
  • Boil rice with salt and whole spices till 70% cooked. Drain.
  • Layer chicken and rice in a pot. Add mint, coriander, fried onions, and saffron milk.
  • Cover and cook on low flame (dum) for 20 minutes. Serve hot.
