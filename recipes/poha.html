Poha Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1 small potato, diced
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Lemon juice and coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  • Rinse poha in water, drain and keep aside.
  • Heat oil, add mustard seeds, peanuts, curry leaves, and chopped potatoes. Fry till golden.
  • Add onions, green chili, and saut├й until soft. Add turmeric.
  • Mix in poha, salt and cook for 3-4 minutes. Turn off the flame.
  • Garnish with coriander and lemon juice. Serve hot.
