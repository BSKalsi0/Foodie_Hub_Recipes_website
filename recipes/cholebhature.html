Chole Bhature Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients (Chole):

  • 1 cup chickpeas (kabuli chana)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Ingredients (Bhature):

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water to knead dough
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  • Soak chickpeas overnight and pressure cook until soft.
  • Heat oil, add cumin, onions, saut├й till golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook.
  • Add tomato puree, spices and cook till oil separates. Add boiled chana and simmer.
  • For bhature, mix all ingredients and knead into a soft dough. Rest for 2 hours.
  • Roll into discs and deep fry until golden and puffed.
  • Serve hot chole with fluffy bhature and salad.
