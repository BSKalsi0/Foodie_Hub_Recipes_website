Mac and Cheese Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g elbow macaroni
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1.5 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp mustard powder (optional)

Instructions:

  • Boil macaroni in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • In a pan, melt butter. Stir in flour to make a roux and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  • Gradually add milk while whisking continuously to avoid lumps.
  • Cook until sauce thickens, then add cheese and stir till melted.
  • Add salt, pepper, and mustard powder (if using). Mix in macaroni and coat well.
  • Serve warm. Optionally bake with breadcrumbs for a crispy top.
