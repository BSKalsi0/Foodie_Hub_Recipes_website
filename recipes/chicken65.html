Chicken 65 Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Curry leaves and green chilies
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 tbsp yogurt (optional for coating)

Instructions:

  • Mix chicken with flours, egg, spices, and ginger garlic paste.
  • Let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  • Heat oil and deep fry chicken until golden and crisp.
  • Saut├й curry leaves and green chilies in a separate pan.
  • Toss fried chicken with saut├йed spices. Serve hot.
