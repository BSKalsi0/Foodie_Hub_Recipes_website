Cheesecake Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g digestive biscuits (crushed)
  • 100g melted butter
  • 400g cream cheese
  • 200ml whipping cream
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fruit topping or sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  • Mix crushed biscuits and melted butter. Press into a springform pan base and chill for 30 mins.
  • In a bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  • In another bowl, whip cream until stiff peaks form. Fold it into the cream cheese mixture.
  • Spread this filling over the biscuit base and level the top. Chill for at least 4тАУ6 hours or overnight.
  • Add fruit topping or sauce before serving. Slice and enjoy!
