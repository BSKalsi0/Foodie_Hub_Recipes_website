Dal Makhani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole black lentils (urad dal)
  • 2 tbsp red kidney beans (rajma)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter or ghee

Instructions:

  • Soak urad dal and rajma overnight and pressure cook until soft.
  • Heat butter, add cumin seeds and chopped onions. Saut├й till golden.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and tomato puree. Cook until oil separates.
  • Add boiled lentils, turmeric, red chili, and salt. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  • Add cream and simmer for another 5 minutes. Serve hot.
