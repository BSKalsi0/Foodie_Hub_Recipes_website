Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  • Cook fettuccine pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • In a pan, melt butter and saut├й garlic until fragrant.
  • Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  • Stir in parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  • Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until coated.
  • Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.
