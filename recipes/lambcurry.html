Lamb Curry Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g lamb, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  • Heat oil in a pan and saut├й onions until golden.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  • Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  • Mix in all spices and cook until oil separates.
  • Add lamb pieces and mix well. Cook for 5-10 minutes.
  • Add water, cover and simmer until lamb is tender.
  • Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
