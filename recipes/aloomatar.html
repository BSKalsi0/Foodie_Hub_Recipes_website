Aloo Matar Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  • Heat oil, add cumin seeds and chopped onions. Saut├й till golden.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  • Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder. Cook until oil separates.
  • Add diced potatoes and peas. Mix well.
  • Add water, cover, and cook till potatoes are soft.
  • Sprinkle garam masala and serve hot with roti or rice.
