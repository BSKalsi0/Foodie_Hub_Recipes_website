Mutton Rogan Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions:

  • Heat oil and saut├й whole spices and onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and fry for a minute.
  • Mix in tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder and cook until oil separates.
  • Add mutton and curd. Mix well and cook for 10 mins on medium heat.
  • Add salt and garam masala, cover and cook until mutton is tender.
  • Serve hot with naan or rice.
