Margherita Pizza

Ingredients (English)

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions (English)

  • Preheat the oven to 220¬∞C (428¬∞F).
  • Spread pizza sauce on the base evenly.
  • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously.
  • Place basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
