Chicken Curry Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (bone-in)
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt and water

Instructions:

  • Heat oil and saut├й onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell goes.
  • Add tomato puree and spices. Cook till oil separates.
  • Add chicken pieces and mix well with the masala.
  • Add water and cook until chicken is tender.
  • Garnish and serve with rice or roti.
