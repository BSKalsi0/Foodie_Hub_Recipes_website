Veg Biryani Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp biryani masala
  • Salt and oil as needed
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves

Instructions:

  • Cook rice till 90% done, drain and keep aside.
  • In oil, add spices, onion, and saut├й till golden.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste, vegetables, tomato, curd, and spices.
  • Cook until veggies are soft and oil separates.
  • Layer rice over masala, sprinkle coriander and mint.
  • Cover and cook on low flame for 10 minutes.
