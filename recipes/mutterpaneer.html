Mutter Paneer Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green peas (mutter)
  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  • Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and onions. Saut├й until golden.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  • Add tomato puree, turmeric, salt and cook till oil separates.
  • Add green peas and a little water. Cook until soft.
  • Add paneer and garam masala. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Serve hot with chapati or rice.
