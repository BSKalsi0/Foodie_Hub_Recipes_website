Kachori Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup moong dal (soaked and ground)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil (for dough)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  • Make a dough using flour, salt, and oil. Rest it for 30 mins.
  • Heat oil, add cumin, fennel, turmeric, and dal paste. Cook till dry.
  • Divide dough and fill with prepared dal stuffing. Flatten slightly.
  • Deep fry on medium flame till golden brown.
  • Serve hot with chutney or aloo sabzi.
