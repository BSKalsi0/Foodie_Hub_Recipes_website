Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  • Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© garlic and onion until golden.
  • Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Add cooked spaghetti and toss to coat in the sauce.
  • Garnish with fresh basil and parmesan. Serve hot.
