Tandoori Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken legs
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  • Make slits in the chicken legs.
  • In a bowl, mix yogurt, spices, lemon juice, and oil.
  • Apply marinade to the chicken and refrigerate for 4тАУ6 hours or overnight.
  • Preheat oven to 200┬░C and bake chicken for 25тАУ30 minutes, turning halfway.
  • Garnish with lemon slices and onions. Serve hot.
