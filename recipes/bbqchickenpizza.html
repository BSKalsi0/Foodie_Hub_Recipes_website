BBQ Chicken Pizza

Ingredients (English)

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup cooked shredded chicken
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • Fresh coriander or parsley for garnish

Instructions (English)

  • Preheat oven to 220┬░C (428┬░F).
  • Spread BBQ sauce over the pizza base.
  • Add shredded chicken evenly.
  • Top with mozzarella cheese and sliced onions.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes until cheese is melted and crust is golden.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander or parsley. Serve hot.
