Shami Kabab Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g minced mutton
  • 1/2 cup chana dal (split Bengal gram)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for shallow frying
  • Fresh coriander and mint (optional)

Instructions:

  • Boil minced meat with chana dal, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and a little water until dry.
  • Grind the mixture into a smooth paste once cooled.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, and garam masala.
  • Shape into round flat kababs.
  • Shallow fry on a hot pan with little oil until golden brown on both sides.
  • Serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.
