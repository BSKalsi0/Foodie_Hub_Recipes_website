Chocolate Mousse Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs (separated)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Instructions:

  • Melt the chocolate and let it cool slightly.
  • Beat egg yolks with sugar and vanilla until creamy.
  • Whisk egg whites in a separate bowl till stiff peaks form.
  • Whip cream until soft peaks form.
  • Fold chocolate into yolk mixture, then add whipped cream and egg whites gently.
  • Pour into serving glasses and refrigerate for 2‚Äì3 hours.
