Veg Korma Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, cauliflower)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp cashew paste
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions:

  • Boil mixed vegetables until soft. Set aside.
  • Heat oil, saut├й onions, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  • Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, salt. Cook well.
  • Add yogurt and cashew paste. Cook until creamy.
  • Mix in boiled vegetables. Simmer 5-7 mins.
  • Sprinkle garam masala, serve hot with naan or rice.
