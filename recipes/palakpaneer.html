Palak Paneer Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups spinach (palak), washed
  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions:

  • Boil spinach for 2-3 minutes, then blend into a smooth paste.
  • Heat oil, add cumin, onions, green chilies, and saut├й until golden.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and tomato puree. Cook until oil separates.
  • Mix in spinach puree and cook for 4-5 minutes.
  • Add paneer cubes, salt, and garam masala. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Serve hot with roti or rice.
