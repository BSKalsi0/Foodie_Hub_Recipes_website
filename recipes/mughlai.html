Chicken Mughlai Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (bone-in preferred)
  • 2 onions, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 10 cashew nuts (soaked and ground)
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions:

  • Heat oil and saut├й onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, coriander powder. Cook well.
  • Add chicken pieces and cook until slightly browned.
  • Mix in curd and cook for 10 minutes on medium heat.
  • Add cashew paste, garam masala, cream, and salt. Cook till chicken is tender.
  • Serve hot with naan or rice.
