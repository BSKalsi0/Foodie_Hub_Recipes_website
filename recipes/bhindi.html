Bhindi Masala Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g ladyfinger (bhindi), sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  • Wash and dry bhindi, then slice it.
  • Heat oil, add cumin, and saut├й onions till golden.
  • Add tomatoes and spices. Cook until soft.
  • Add bhindi and stir well. Cook on low flame till tender.
  • Garnish with coriander and serve hot with chapati.
