Rogan Josh Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp fennel powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt as per taste

Instructions:

  • Heat ghee and saut├й onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  • Add mutton and sear until light brown.
  • Add whisked yogurt and spices. Mix well.
  • Cover and cook on low flame until mutton is tender.
  • Sprinkle garam masala and serve hot with naan or rice.
