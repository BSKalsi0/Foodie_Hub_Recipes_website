Rasmalai Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter milk (for making chenna)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 liter full-fat milk (for rabri)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands
  • Chopped pistachios and almonds for garnish
  • 2 cups water (for sugar syrup)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for syrup)

Instructions:

  • Boil 1 liter milk and add lemon juice to curdle. Strain to get chenna. Wash and squeeze well.
  • Knead the chenna for 5тАУ7 minutes to make a smooth dough. Make small flat discs.
  • Boil 2 cups water with 1/2 cup sugar. Add discs and cook for 10тАУ12 minutes. They will puff up.
  • In another pan, boil 1 liter milk till reduced to half. Add sugar, cardamom, saffron, and dry fruits.
  • Squeeze cooked discs and soak them in prepared rabri. Chill for a few hours before serving.
