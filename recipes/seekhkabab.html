Seekh Kabab Recipe

ЁЯЗмЁЯЗз English Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced meat (lamb/chicken)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for grilling or frying

Instructions:

  • Mix all ingredients in a bowl thoroughly.
  • Shape the mixture onto skewers like long sausages.
  • Grill or fry until browned and cooked through.
  • Serve hot with chutney or onion rings.
